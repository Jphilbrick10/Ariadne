- teaching and coursework

**You do not need to pay or ask for anything to use Ariadne for those purposes.**
Use it, fork it, build on it, publish results from it. We only ask that you keep
the copyright/notice intact (the license requires it).

## When you need a commercial license

You need a separate, paid commercial license if you use Ariadne — in whole or in
part, modified or unmodified — for **commercial advantage**, including:

- inside a product or service you sell, host, or monetize
- internal use at a for-profit company as part of doing business
- consulting or paid analysis delivered to clients
- any use whose purpose is commercial gain

If you are not sure which side of the line you fall on, ask — we would rather have
a quick conversation than leave you guessing.

## How to get one

Email **weareone@oneunity.earth** with a short description of how you would like to
use Ariadne. Terms (flat fee, subscription, or revenue-based) are set per use case.
Startups, small teams, and good-faith open-source projects are welcome to ask about
reduced or waived terms.
