Ariadne is source-available under the [PolyForm Noncommercial License 1.0.0](LICENSE).
This note states how that license applies to AI/ML use, because the question comes up
often and the answer is not always obvious.

## Non-commercial AI/ML research: permitted

Using Ariadne (its code, data, or outputs) for **non-commercial** machine-learning
research, study, or experimentation is permitted under the noncommercial license, the
same as any other non-commercial use.

## Commercial AI/ML development: requires a license

Using Ariadne, in whole or in part, to **train, fine-tune, evaluate, distill, or
otherwise develop** an AI/ML model, dataset, or system for **commercial purposes**
requires a separate commercial license. This includes, without limitation:

- training or fine-tuning a model that is sold, hosted, or otherwise monetized
- building a commercial product, service, or dataset that incorporates Ariadne's code or outputs
- use by a for-profit company, or the for-profit arm, subsidiary, or affiliate of a
  non-profit, for commercial advantage

Being a non-profit does not by itself grant commercial rights; what matters is the
**purpose** of the use.
